Carrie (Ha Eun) Park

Ph.D. Candidate in Political Science and International Affairs
Department of Political Science and International Affairs
University of Georgia

I study international organizations, human rights, and international institutions. My dissertation examines how international organizations reflect and reshape interstate power dynamics, with a focus on voting rules, compliance behavior, and the political economy of intergovernmental cooperation. I draw on network analysis, time series methods, and formal modeling to study how states interact within multilateral institutions.
